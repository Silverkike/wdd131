What is 911?

The 911 Emergency Call Center is a national service operated by the Polic√≠a Nacional Civil de El Salvador. We Receive

Our operators are available 24/7 to answer emergency calls. They collect vital information including location, nature of the emergency, and urgency level to ensure accurate and timely response.

We Coordinate

Using advanced dispatch systems, we connect with police, medical, and fire units. Coordination is key to deploying the right resources to the right place at the right time.

We Respond

Emergency units arrive on scene guided by real-time information. Our goal is to minimize response time and maximize effectiveness in saving lives and securing communities.
