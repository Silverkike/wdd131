911 PNC Emergency Call Center – Website Planning Document

1. Site Name

Site Name: 911 PNC Emergency Call Center

This name represents the emergency call center operated by the National Civil Police of El Salvador. It is a recognizable and authoritative name that reflects the site's purpose: to inform and educate the public about the vital role of the 911 system in public safety.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to raise awareness and educate the public about the operations, responsibilities, and importance of the 911 Emergency Call Center in El Salvador. It will provide:

  • An overview of how emergency calls are handled.
  • Profiles of the professional operators.
  • Statistics and performance indicators.
  • Guidelines for proper use of the service.
  • A contact form for suggestions or non-urgent reports.

3. Scenarios

  • How does the 911 call center handle emergency calls and what happens after I call?
  • What kind of training or qualifications do 911 operators have?

4. Color Schema

Midnight Blue (#003366): Used for headers, navigation bar, and footer background to convey professionalism and authority.

Safety Yellow (#FFD700): Used for call-to-action buttons, icons, and highlights to draw attention and reflect urgency.

Light Gray (#C5C8CF): Used for text areas to ensure content readability and a clean look.

5. Typography

Roboto: Used for body text and paragraphs for readability and modern appearance.

Oswald: Used for headings and titles to provide a bold, structured look.
